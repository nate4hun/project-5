Best Nintendo Switch Games

Zelda: Breath of the Wild

Zelda: Breath of the Wild is an open-world action-adventure game developed and published by Nintendo. It offers a vast, beautiful world to explore and a compelling story to experience.

Video Source: YouTube

Luigi's Mansion 3

Luigi's Mansion 3 is a charming and spooky adventure game where Luigi explores a haunted hotel to rescue his friends. The game is filled with clever puzzles and entertaining ghost-catching action.

Video Source: YouTube

Cuphead

Cuphead is a classic run and gun action game heavily focused on boss battles. Inspired by cartoons of the 1930s, the visuals and audio are meticulously created with the same techniques of the era.
